Credit System

Understand how credits work for hiring specialists

  • Purchase credits to hire specialists
  • Credits are deducted when you accept a proposal
  • Unused credits remain in your account
  • Refill credits anytime from your profile
  • Credits can be refunded when candidates are rejected

Order Cancellation

Cancel orders and applications when needed

  • Go to Orders tab
  • Select the order you want to cancel
  • Tap 'Cancel Order'
  • Confirm cancellation
  • Note: Cancellation policies may vary by order type
  • Orders can only be canceled if no specialist has been hired
